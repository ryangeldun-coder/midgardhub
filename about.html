About Midgard Community Hub

Your complete fan-made database for Ragnarok Zero Global

Hi, I'm Ryan Geldun!

I'm a passionate Ragnarok Online player and content creator. I built Midgard Community Hub because I wanted one place where players of Ragnarok Zero Global could find accurate, up-to-date game data without jumping between wiki pages and forum posts.

What is Midgard Community Hub?

Midgard Community Hub is a free, community-driven fan website for Ragnarok Online — specifically the Ragnarok Zero Global server (based on TWRo Zero). It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Gravity Co., Ltd. or any official game publisher.

The site provides players with:

  • 📖 Monster Database – full stats, drops, and spawn locations
  • ⚔️ Item Database – weapons, armor, cards, consumables
  • 🔧 Refine Simulator – calculate refine success rates and costs
  • ⚒️ Forge & Brew Calculators – for blacksmiths and alchemists
  • 🗺️ Memorial Dungeon Guides – strategies and boss info
  • 📚 Lore & Guides – game lore, class builds, and tips

Why I Made This Site

When I started playing Ragnarok Zero Global, I struggled to find reliable English-language resources. Most data was locked in Taiwanese forums or scattered across Discord threads. I started building this database for my YouTube community, and it grew into a full-featured hub that thousands of players use every day.

Everything here is maintained by me and the amazing community around the Ryan Geldun YouTube channel. Your support keeps this site alive!

Contact & Community

Have a question, found incorrect data, or want to contribute? The best way to reach me is through our community Discord server. You can also find me on YouTube where I regularly post Ragnarok guides and gameplay.
