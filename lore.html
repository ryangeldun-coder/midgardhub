About the Ragnarok Zero Lore Archives

Explore the rich history of the world with the Ragnarok Zero Lore Archives. Curated by Ryan Geldun, our collection of video deep-dives covers everything from the dark origins of the Bio Labs to the mythologies of Midgard's most iconic bosses. This archive covers Ragnarok Zero Global(also known as TWRo Zero or RO Zero Global) — the international version of Taiwan's Ragnarok Online Zero. Understanding the story behind the game adds a new layer of depth to your journey through the world of Midgard. Our archives are constantly updated with new research and storytelling, with all episodes available since March 2026.
