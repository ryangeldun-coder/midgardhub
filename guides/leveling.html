Leveling Guide: 1 → 50

TWROZ

Optimal path — quests, grind spots & dailies

TWRoZ Exclusive: Free stat & skill resets until Lv. 40 — experiment freely. Build full AGI for the 1–40 grind phase, then reset to your endgame stats at Lv. 39. Quest EXP rates are higher than in classic RO — prioritize quest chains before pure grinding.

Lv. 1 – 15
Awakening
Prontera & Surroundings

Start by completing the Paradise Group (PG) tutorial quest chain — it hands out free gear (Paradise Dagger or Sword) that will carry you well past Lv. 20. After collecting your PG set, grind on Prontera field mobs and complete Izlude Academy introductory quests for bonus EXP. The PG dailies (available at Lv. 5) are worth doing every day since they give Zelunium Ore alongside EXP.

Tips
  • Collect the Paradise Group starter pack first — it's free gear that outperforms anything you can buy early.
  • TWRoZ: free stat/skill resets are available until Lv. 40 — don't worry about your starting build.

  • Mission Board at Prontera (Lv. 10+) — rotating kill/fetch tasks
Lv. 15 – 30
Regional Expansion
Payon · Geffen · Morocc

At Lv. 15 job-change into your 1st class and travel to Payon or Geffen. Both cities have dense quest chains that give substantial EXP in TWRoZ. Payon Cave is the most efficient grind spot for Lv. 18–25 (Spores, Zombies). Then push through Geffen Region for Orc quests (Lv. 25+). The Morocc chain unlocks a good travel reward once you reach that area. Daily quests from Payon and Geffen Mission Boards become very important here.

Lv. 30 – 42
The Deep Grind
Mjolnir · Byalan · Alberta

This is the longest and most gear-dependent phase. Mjolnir Mountains hosts the Malani quest chain — a long story chain worth a lot of EXP but requiring exploration across a large field. Byalan Dungeon (Izlude Island) is the most efficient AFK-grind spot for Lv. 30–40 (Marse, Vadon, Obeaune). Alberta unlocks the Turtle Island chain which gives a significant EXP boost. By Lv. 35 you should be working toward 2nd class job change.

Tips
  • Byalan is the best pure-grind zone — spawn rate is high and there's no field interference.

  • TWRoZ: Byalan Dungeon has a Fever Map event version — watch the server calendar for 200% EXP Fever windows.
  • Alberta's Turtle Island chain is gated behind a pre-quest — speak to the Ouch Taiya NPC first.

Lv. 42 – 50
Pre-Transcendence Push
Glast Heim · Aldebaran · Clock Tower

The final stretch before level 50 and your 2nd class peak. Glast Heim is the premier leveling area — the outer map alone (gl_fild) has Raydrics and Khalitzburg at very high density. Complete the Glast Heim prison and church quest chains for strong story EXP. Clock Tower in Aldebaran is optional but has dense mob spawns if you prefer a change of scenery. By Lv. 50 you should be ready to grind toward job level 50 for 2nd class breakthrough (transcendence).

Tips
  • Raydric on glast_01 is among the best EXP/hour at Lv. 42–50 for any melee class.

  • Glast Heim prison (gl_prison) is denser but harder — recommended at Lv. 46+.
  • TWRoZ: The Glast Heim Epic Quest unlocks the Himmelmez Memorial Dungeon, the source of best Lv. 50–60 shadow gear.
  • Clock Tower is great for solo Hunters/Snipers — bring Trap items, mobs cluster tightly.
  • By Lv. 48–50, start stockpiling 1000x Oridecon Hammer materials for your 2nd class transcendence gear push.

After Lv. 50 — What's Next?

Job Level 50 → 2nd Class
Priority #1. Job-change ASAP — your 2nd class skills are a massive damage multiplier.
Glast Heim Epic Quest
TWRoZ exclusive story chain. Unlocks Himmelmez Memorial Dungeon with best Lv. 50–60 shadow gear.
Battle Doll System
Farm daily Battle Dolls from mobs. Exchange for God Metal Ore — critical for +7 shadow gear refinement.
Fever Map Events
Check the calendar. Fever windows on Glast Heim / Byalan give 200% EXP — plan your grind sessions around them.
