System Guide

PVP Maps — The Hidden Realm

A group of entry-level NPCs wearing smiling masks, calling themselves the "Mysterious Guide", will invite any adventurer holding a Hidden Realm Key to venture into a PVP-flagged version of the map — boosted spawns, boosted rewards, real risk.

"It's a place filled with #Wealth #Conflict" — "Field PvP Map Added" — #Monster respawn speed UP!! UP!! #Monster count UP #Drop rate UP #EXP UP

Hidden Realm Rules

It's a PVP map — everyone who isn't in your party, guild, or alliance counts as an enemy.

Monster count and respawn speed are 1.5x the original map's.

Using your Hidden Realm Key inside grants a 12-hour exclusive buff — +10% EXP on Ragnarok Zero Global, lost the moment you leave the map (TWRoZ source states +15% EXP & drop rate; drop rate change unconfirmed for Global).

Logging out or disconnecting inside returns you to your saved position the next time you log in.

The Hidden Realm purges itself daily at 00:00 — everyone is returned to their original channel.
