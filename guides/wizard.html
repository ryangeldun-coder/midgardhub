RAGNAROK ZERO GLOBAL

Mastering the Elements and the Meta

AoE Destruction

The Master of Screen Clearing

The classic Wizard playstyle. This build focuses on maximizing the damage of Storm Gust, Meteor Storm, and Lord of Vermilion to clear entire dungeons in seconds. Ideal for power-leveling and party mobbing.

Optimal Stat Spread

INT: 99

Maximized MATK and SP pool. Mandatory for high-level spell scaling.

DEX: 80-99

Reduces long variable cast times for your big AoE spells.

VIT: 30-50

Essential for surviving mob hits while you are casting.

LUK: Remaining

Minor MATK boost and status resistance.

Priority Skills

Storm Gust (Lv. 10)

Your primary farming and CC tool. The freeze rate is essential for safety.

Meteor Storm (Lv. 10)

Top tier AoE damage against Earth and Undead targets.

Gear Strategy

AoE Arsenal

  • Eden Staff III: Base weapon. Look for Cast Time reduction stones.
  • Shadow Wizard Armor: Increases Storm Gust and LoV damage.
  • Shadow Wizard Shoes: Reduces the cast time of AoE spells.

MATK Stacking

  • Mage Coat [1]: Provides much needed INT and MDEF.
  • Survivor's Rod [1]: Paired with Survivor's Manteau for tankiness.
  • Isilla Card: Chance to reduce cast time significantly on hit.
PRO VETERAN TIP

TWROZ map tier list for AoE Wizard (confirmed by auto-battle farming streams): Centipede area → Geffen Tower 2F → Mine 3F → Odin's Shrine 2F. Odin's Shrine 2F is the benchmark — your setup is competitive if it beats that map's clear rate.
