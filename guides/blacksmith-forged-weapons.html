New Blacksmith Forged Weapons

The 2026/04/08 maintenance opened a brand new series of Blacksmith-forged weapons (鐵匠全新鍛造系列武器) — 9 weapons spanning every melee weapon type, all sharing the same Weapon Level 3 / Base Level 60 requirement and a matching refine-bonus structure.

Refine +9 mechanic

At Refine +9, each weapon simultaneously includes its Refine +7bonus — i.e. the two damage bonuses stack (see totals in each card below). The official patch notes mention the in-item description text didn't originally reflect this and was scheduled to be corrected in a later maintenance; the mechanic itself was already active from launch.

The same maintenance also opened "Undead Ruler of the Sunken Ship" — a Level 90 instance with a hidden phase: after clearing the Storm Pirate King, a random number of Storm Evil Chests attempt to flee; killing all of them (once 6+ have spawned) unlocks a secret passage to a true treasure room. That room is a point-of-no-return area — bring a Butterfly Wingbefore entering, since there's no way back until the instance timer runs out. A follow-up maintenance later fixed a display bug where the Sunset Busteraxe wasn't rendering its weapon appearance correctly.
