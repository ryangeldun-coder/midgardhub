Upgrade — The Ornate Metal Rod

"Ornate Metal Fishing Rod?! A blue Concentrated EXP Syrup?!"

Ornate Metal Fishing Rod — Cash Shop
12 hours120 Cash
6 hours60 Cash
1 hour10 Cash
Can be selected for use as the "Ornate Metal Fishing Rod" at any fishing spot. No bait required — unlimited uses within the time limit.

EXP Syrup

Drinking it grants 40,000 Base EXP. A single use raises Base Level by at most 1.

Required Lv 50, Limit Lv 99 · Weight 0 · Untradeable

Concentrated EXP Syrup

Drinking it grants 40,000 Base EXP (concentrated variant).

Required Lv 50, Limit Lv 99 · Weight 0 · Untradeable

JOB Syrup

Using it grants 3,000 Job EXP.

Required Lv 5, Limit Lv 49 · Weight 0 · Untradeable

Concentrated JOB Syrup

Using it grants 8,000 Job EXP.

Required Lv 50, Limit Lv 99 · Weight 0 · Untradeable

Note: syrup also can't be used in certain beginner areas or indoors.
