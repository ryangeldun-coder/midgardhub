Zeny Farming Guide

AFK Spots · Active Grinding · Merchant Strategies

AFK spot data sourced from testing on TWRo Zero (TW server) with high server population. Item prices and income vary between servers — treat this as a guide to what to farm and why, not a zeny-per-hour guarantee.

AFK Farming Spots

1
Strawberry Fields
Maze Forest / various fieldsRogue (auto-steal)Beginner
Strawberry

Strawberries are a cooking ingredient with consistent player demand. Rogue's auto-steal passive makes this one of the highest-yield AFK spots relative to setup difficulty. Set your auto-loot to only pick up Strawberries and a few high-value misc drops — a clean loot filter makes a big difference over a long session.

Rogue only — auto-steal boosts yield significantly
Filter loot list to avoid inventory overflow
Best beginner AFK spot: easy to set up, consistent income
2
Byalan Dungeon
Izlude Dungeon (iz_dun floors)AnyBeginner–Intermediate
Mermaid's Heart

Mermaid's Heart drops at 45% from Marc (Mermaid) mobs in Byalan Dungeon. It's a crafting and quest material that moves well on the player market. Misc drops from surrounding mobs — Gill, Fin, Solid Shell — add steady NPC vendor income alongside the Hearts. Bring a Kafra Bell for remote storage: the dungeon is far from the nearest bank.

Mermaid's Heart: 45% drop from Marc — always list on player market
Kafra Bell essential — dungeon is far from storage
Misc drops (Gill, Fin, Solid Shell) go straight to NPC
3
Ant Hell
Ant Hell dungeon (an_d / anthel maps)AnyIntermediate
Chain Hammer

Chain Hammer drops from Piere (Worker Ant, lv35) in Ant Hell. Weapons are heavy and fill inventory quickly — plan for frequent storage runs or bring max weight reduction gear. Ant Hell has high mob density which makes kills per hour strong, but weight management is the main challenge for long AFK sessions.

Target: Piere (lv35 Worker Ant) for Chain Hammer
Weapons are heavy — manage inventory actively
High density spawn compensates for lower individual drop value
4
Pyramid F1
Morroc Pyramid Floor 1 (moc_pyramid)AnyBeginner
Immortal Heart

Immortal Heart drops at 45% from Mummy Dog on Pyramid F1. Always list it on the player market through your vending stall — player demand is much higher than NPC value. Misc drops go to NPC. Solid passive income once set up, but the pyramid is very crowded during peak server hours.

Immortal Heart: player market only, not NPC
Very crowded at peak hours — go off-peak for better spawn
Misc drops to NPC, Hearts to market stall

General AFK Tips

Use Rogue for passive farms
Auto-steal passive adds bonus drops on top of the listed drop rate. Even a 45% drop mob becomes significantly more profitable with Rogue.
Configure your loot filter
Only pick up target items and a few high-value misc drops. Full inventory means you stop farming. Get this right before starting a session.
Weapons need active management
Weapon-drop spots (Ant Hell, New-Grade) earn the most but weapons are heavy. You need frequent storage runs or gear optimized for max weight.
Off-peak for popular spots
Pyramid F1 and Orc-area maps get crowded at peak hours. Log in at off-peak times for better spawn access.
Stack nearby daily quests
Many farming areas have daily quests nearby. Completing them while AFK adds income with zero extra time invested.
List Hearts on market, not NPC
Immortal Heart and Mermaid's Heart have strong player demand. Always vend these through your market stall, never NPC.

Merchant Strategies

Merchant Alt — Overcharge & Discount

Keep a Merchant alt always. Overcharge sells to NPC for +24%, Discount buys from NPC for -24%. The savings compound on every transaction. Essential for serious farmers.

Offline Vending

List your loot in Prontera and let it sell while you're offline. Undercut the cheapest listing by 1z to appear first. Re-price each session — the market moves daily.

Never Blind-Sell to NPC

Random Options on gear can make any drop worth 10x–50x NPC price. Check the market before vendoring anything with stats. One good roll can fund a whole day of farming.

Greed Blacksmith

Blacksmith Greed auto-loots everything in range. In Fever Maps where players skip trash drops, a Greed BS picks up every low-value item. Volume adds up over long sessions.

Kafra Bell

Remote storage access so you never walk back to the bank. Essential for any AFK spot far from a city — especially Byalan Dungeon and Ant Hell.

Bottom line: Strawberry Fields is the easiest entry point. Byalan Dungeon and Ant Hell offer better long-term income once you understand loot management. Pyramid F1 works well as a background farm with Immortal Hearts on the market. Always run a Merchant alt with a vending stall — passive market income compounds everything.
