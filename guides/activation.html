Activation System

Upgrade ★ equipment to ★★ — preserving cards, enchants & random options

How it works: Collect Forging Certificates by trading monster-drop gear to NPC Nariya, then spend them at Grandpa Taotao to activate (upgrade tier) your ★ weapons and classic armor. Cards, enchants, and random options are always preserved — only the refine level drops.

NPC Locations — Prontera

Workshop Apprentice Nariya
Prontera 272, 264
Certificate Exchange
  • Exchange any monster-drop equipment (1 pc) → 1× ★ Forging Certificate (matching type)
  • Exchange an already-activated ★ weapon → 1× ★★ Forging Certificate (matching type) ★ New
Grandpa Taotao
Prontera 272, 260
Activation Interface
  • Collect the required certificates + Goblin Coin Fragments, then talk to him to open the upgrade interface.

Practical Example — ★★ Jur (Assassin Katar)

Assassin Cross end-game weapon

The ★★ Jur used in the 190 ASPD Sonic Blow build is a ★★ Katar — meaning it has already been through the activation process. To get one you either buy it from the market, or:

1

Farm monster-drop Katars and trade them to NPC Nariya for ★ Katar Forging Certificates.

2

Collect 100× ★ Katar Certificates + 8× Goblin Coin Fragments.

3

Talk to Grandpa Taotao — select your ★ Jur as the source weapon.

4

Result: ★★ Jur with refine −7 (so push it to +9 first → ends at +2), cards/enchants fully preserved.

5

NEW route: if you already have a ★★ Jur, exchange it to NPC Nariya for ★★ Certificates, then use the −3 rule (3× cert + 30 Goblin Coin Fragments) to tier it up further with less refine loss.

See also: Assassin Guide — 190 ASPD Sonic Blow for the full stat build that uses the ★★ Jur.
