About Midgard Scout

Welcome to the ultimate **Ragnarok Online geoguesser** experience. Midgard Scout is a fan-made interactive challenge designed to test your spatial memory of the world of Midgard. From the hidden paths of Mjolnir to the deepest levels of Glast Heim, our **RO geoguesser** features over 100 high-quality screenshots. Identify locations using their system map names and see if you have what it takes to earn the title of Legendary Scout.
