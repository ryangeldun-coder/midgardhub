Dungeon Mechanics

1
Linear instance — clear waves of Enhanced Porings (Poring, Drops, Poporing, Marin) to break barriers and open the next area.
2
Three bosses spawn in sequence: Giant Goldring (Holy 1, 72K HP) → Giant Amering (Poison 1, 72K HP) → King Poring (Neutral 2, 140K HP).
3
Blue Pillars of Light are scattered across the map — step into one for a 60-second buff granting +20 all stats and increased movement speed.
4
Porings are naturally attracted to items dropped on the ground — exploit this to clump them for AoE kills.
5
King Poring summons minion Porings during the fight that reduce your Flee rate and amplify incoming damage — clear them quickly.

Pro Tips

Green Onion and Radish headgears drop with a chance from the dungeon — when used they can transform the wearer (and nearby players), which can disrupt gameplay if used mid-fight.
Giant Goldring is Holy 1 — Shadow/Dark element weapons deal 200% damage. Giant Amering is Poison 1 — Earth element weapons deal 200%.
King Poring is Neutral 2 — no elemental weakness; focus on raw ATK/MATK output.
Collect Jello Fragments daily and combine 5 of the same type into a Jello Stone to craft Memorial Dungeon gear.
